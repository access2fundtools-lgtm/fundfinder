🌍 About This Opportunity

The MEST AI Startup Program is MEST Africa's flagship initiative to identify and back the next generation of African AI entrepreneurs. Now accepting applications for the Class of 2027, the programme offers one of the most comprehensive support packages on the continent — all at no cost to participants.

Selected founders receive a fully-funded 12-month residency in Accra, Ghana. This includes programme fees, accommodation, flights from your home country, and a monthly living stipend. The programme splits into 7 months of intensive AI training and 4 months of incubation to build and launch your startup.

Upon graduation, teams pitch for up to $100,000 in pre-seed investment from MEST's dedicated fund, along with connections to their global investor network spanning the US, Europe, and across Africa. Nigerian entrepreneurs are explicitly welcomed to apply.

🎁 What's Fully Covered

  • All programme tuition fees — 100% paid by MEST
  • Return flights from Nigeria (or your home country) to Accra, Ghana
  • Accommodation in Accra for the duration of the programme
  • Monthly living stipend to cover day-to-day expenses
  • Access to co-working space, equipment, and cloud compute credits
  • Mentorship from 150+ AI experts, investors, and founders globally

👤 Who Qualifies

  • AI entrepreneurs or aspiring AI entrepreneurs from Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, or Kenya
  • Strong interest in building AI-powered products and startups
  • Early-stage — a working idea is preferred, MVP not required
  • Willing to relocate to Accra, Ghana for 12 months
  • Passionate about solving African challenges using artificial intelligence
  • Both individuals and co-founding teams may apply

📝 How to Apply

  • Apply via the MEST SMAply portal (link below)
  • Complete the online application — describe your AI startup idea or current venture
  • Submit a short video introduction (usually 2–3 minutes)
  • Selected applicants progress to interview rounds
  • Final cohort announcement made several weeks after the deadline
  • Deadline: July 20, 2026 — do not wait until the last day
