🌍 About This Opportunity

The BATN Foundation Farmers for the Future (F4F) programme is one of Nigeria's most recognised agripreneur grant competitions, run by the British American Tobacco Nigeria (BATN) Foundation. The 2026 edition is now open and invites Nigeria's brightest young minds in agriculture to compete for cash prizes totalling over ₦10 million.

The programme was launched to address Nigeria's food security challenges by identifying and empowering the next generation of agricultural entrepreneurs. It targets youth, including NYSC members serving across Nigeria, who are pursuing commercially viable and scalable agricultural ventures.

Beyond the cash, winners receive mentorship, access to BATN Foundation's network of agribusiness experts, and significant media visibility — all of which can help accelerate their ventures far beyond the prize itself.

👤 Who Qualifies

  • Young Nigerians — typically aged 18–35 (confirm exact age range on portal)
  • NYSC members currently serving anywhere in Nigeria
  • Agribusiness owners or those with a concrete, viable agricultural business plan
  • Applicants engaged in any segment of Nigeria's agricultural value chain
  • Nigerian citizens only — this programme is exclusively for Nigerians
  • Both individuals and small teams may be eligible to apply

📝 How to Apply

  • Visit the BATN Foundation portal: batnf.net/wealthishere
  • Register and complete the online application form
  • Describe your agribusiness venture — current stage, impact, and scalability
  • Upload required documents (ID, business plan, photos/evidence of your venture)
  • Application is free — no fee at any stage
  • Apply as soon as possible — deadline not published but the window is competitive
