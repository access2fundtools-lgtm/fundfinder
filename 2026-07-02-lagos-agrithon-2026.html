About This Programme

The Lagos Agrithon 2026 is the flagship youth agrifood innovation and enterprise acceleration programme of the Lagos State Ministry of Agriculture and Food Systems, powered by the Lagos Agrinnovation Club. This year's edition offers access to a ₦200 million grant pool to support innovative agribusinesses and agritech solutions transforming Lagos State's food systems. Selected participants also receive intensive mentorship, market access support, and connections to investors and buyers across Nigeria.
