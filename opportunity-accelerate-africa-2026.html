🌍 About This Opportunity

Accelerate Africa is a flagship programme of the Nigeria Startup Act ecosystem, designed to identify and invest in the most promising early-stage African startups. Partnered with the Future Africa Fund, the 2026 cohort offers investment between $250,000 and $500,000 for a small, curated group of just 10 founders.

Beyond the investment, participants receive 12 weeks of intensive support — expert mentorship, market access workshops, connections to global investors and partners, and hands-on guidance from Africa's most experienced operators. This is not a grant: it's equity investment in founders who are building scalable solutions.

The programme is open to African founders, with Nigerian entrepreneurs fully eligible to apply. Applications are completely free, and no equity is required to go through the application process.

Note: This is equity investment, not a grant. Investors take a stake in your company. The exact investment amount depends on stage, traction, and pitch performance.

👤 Who Qualifies

  • Early-stage African startup founders — Nigerian founders explicitly welcome
  • Must have at least 2 co-founders (solo founders typically not eligible)
  • Startup should be solving a scalable problem with African and global market potential
  • Product or service in any sector — tech, fintech, health, agri, edtech, climate
  • Based in Africa (physical presence on the continent required)
  • Some level of early traction preferred but not required at all stages

🎁 What You Get

  • $250,000 to $500,000 in equity investment from the Future Africa Fund
  • 12-week structured accelerator programme with expert mentorship
  • Access to the Accelerate Africa and Nigeria Startup Act investor network
  • Global partnerships and market access opportunities
  • Demo Day pitch to additional investors for follow-on funding
  • Post-programme support and continued advisory connections

📝 How to Apply

  • Visit the Nigeria Startup Act / Accelerate Africa portal (link below)
  • Create a profile and complete the online application form
  • Describe your startup, market size, traction, and founding team
  • Shortlisted founders will be contacted for interviews
  • Final cohort of 10 founders announced after selection process
  • Deadline: July 25, 2026 — submit early, applications reviewed on a rolling basis
