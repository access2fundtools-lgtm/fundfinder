# Cowork Task — FundFinder AI: Program Principal (Supply-Side) Module

**Project:** FundFinder AI | A2F Partners
**Assigned to:** @DayoAkin / A2F Strategy
**Phase:** 2 (runs after base platform foundation)

---

## Brief

Expand FundFinder AI beyond applicant-facing discovery into a full two-sided platform. The supply side — organisations running grants, empowerment programs, credit schemes, government funds, and similar capital deployment programs (termed **"Program Principals"** on the platform) — should be able to register, onboard, and use FundFinder AI to fully administer their programs end-to-end.

---

## Scope

### 1. Registration & Onboarding Portal
Program Principals self-register, verify organisation credentials, and configure their program parameters:
- Eligibility criteria
- Funding size and disbursement structure
- Timelines
- Geography

### 2. API-Connected Agent Layer
A dedicated A2F AI agent manages the entire program lifecycle on their behalf:
- Application intake
- Eligibility screening
- Shortlisting and scoring
- Selection recommendations
- Applicant communication
- Disbursement tracking
- Post-funding reporting

### 3. NGX Growth Board Consolidation Pathway
A2F Partners serves as the institutional bridge: qualifying portfolio companies funded through programs on the platform are consolidated and prepared for listing or registration on the NGX Growth Board.

This positions A2F as the **capital markets offtake partner** for every program that runs through FundFinder AI.

---

## Key Design Principles

- Program Principals experience **zero manual overhead** — the agent handles all program operations via API
- The platform supports all capital types: grants, soft loans, empowerment funds, credit guarantees, blended finance
- The NGX Growth Board pathway is surfaced to Program Principals **at onboarding** as a value-added outcome — not an afterthought
