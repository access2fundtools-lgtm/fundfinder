Eligibility Requirements

  • SME with 10–250 full-time employees
  • Annual revenue between $250,000 and $10 million USD
  • Registered and operating in an eligible African country (Nigeria qualifies)
  • Minimum 3 years of operating history
  • Demonstrated growth potential and plans to scale
  • Willing to undergo IFC due diligence and business assessment

Who Should NOT Apply

  • Pre-revenue startups or idea-stage businesses
  • Businesses with fewer than 10 employees
  • Businesses with annual revenue below $250,000
  • Public sector / government-owned enterprises

What You Get

  • Up to $1.5M in combined grant, TA (technical assistance), and potential equity investment
  • IFC global advisory services — operations, HR, finance, governance
  • Access to IFC's global SME network spanning 100+ countries
  • Pathway to larger IFC financing for subsequent growth rounds
  • International credibility that opens doors to DFI and institutional investors

About This Programme

The IFC SME Growth Accelerator is the International Finance Corporation's flagship enterprise development programme for high-growth SMEs across emerging markets. Unlike startup grants, this programme targets established businesses ready to scale. IFC deploys a blend of grant capital, technical assistance, and investment readiness support — with the goal of helping selected SMEs double revenue, create jobs, and build systems for sustainable growth. Nigerian businesses in manufacturing, agribusiness, health, fintech, and services are particularly encouraged. The November 15 deadline gives you time to build a strong application — start now.
