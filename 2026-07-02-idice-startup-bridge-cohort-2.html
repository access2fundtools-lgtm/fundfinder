About This Programme

The iDICE Startup Bridge Founders Lab is a Federal Government-backed initiative under the Investment in Digital and Creative Enterprises (iDICE) programme, implemented by the Bank of Industry (BOI). Cohort 2 opens June 24 and closes July 26, 2026. 125 founders will be selected to join a 12-week intensive that combines mentorship, training, and funding milestones — with the best 100 receiving ₦10 million each in non-dilutive grants. This is real government money, zero equity taken.
