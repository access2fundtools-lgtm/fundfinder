## The Five Growth Suite Modules (Entrepreneur-Facing — Free)

### 1. Supplier Connect
AI analyses the entrepreneur's business profile, sector, spend patterns, and procurement needs → surfaces ranked supplier matches with risk scores. Not a directory — a contextual recommendation engine.

**Revenue:** Suppliers pay to be verified and listed (₦50,000–₦500,000/year per tier).

### 2. Contract Radar
Pulls live tender opportunities from BPP, state procurement portals, NCDMB local content registers, and ministry notices. Matches against the company's CAC registration, sector, capacity, and compliance status. Flags readiness gaps. Entrepreneur sees only what they can realistically win.

**Revenue:** Government agencies and procurement bodies pay for verified vendor pipeline access.

### 3. Growth Partners
Recommends the most relevant PR firms, media buyers, influencers, trade promoters, and B2B marketing outfits based on sector, product type, and budget range — ranked with context.

**Revenue:** Promoters and marketers pay to be verified and listed (B2B lead generation marketplace).

### 4. Risk Desk
For every objective the entrepreneur sets — enter new market, sign supplier contract, apply for government contract, hire, deploy grant funds — the AI runs a structured risk assessment: execution risk, financial exposure, regulatory compliance risk, counterparty risk, timeline risk. Scores each and produces a plain-language brief the entrepreneur approves before acting.

**Revenue:** Trust-builder. The feature that creates deep platform stickiness.

### 5. Outreach Engine (Pro — Locked)
Entrepreneur selects a target (supplier, contract, promoter, funder). AI drafts the email, DM, WhatsApp message, or formal proposal. Entrepreneur reads, approves, platform sends. Zero typing.

**Rule:** No message sends without entrepreneur approval. Always.
